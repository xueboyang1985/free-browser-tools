# Free Browser Tools

Curated list of free browser-based tools that run 100% locally — no upload, no signup, no server.

## Tools

### 📖 [Kindle Highlights Exporter](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/kindle-exporter/)
Export your Kindle highlights and notes to Markdown, CSV, or JSON. Drag and drop My Clippings.txt and export in your preferred format.
- [Live Tool](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/kindle-exporter/)
- [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/xueboyang1985/kindle-exporter)
- [User Guide](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/kindle-exporter/guide.html)

### 🎬 [Subtitle Converter & Transcript Exporter](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/subtitle-converter/)
Convert SRT, VTT, and ASS subtitles to TXT, Markdown, CSV, or JSON. Preview with timestamps and export as structured data.
- [Live Tool](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/subtitle-converter/)
- [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/xueboyang1985/subtitle-converter)
- [User Guide](https://xueboyang1985.github.io/subtitle-converter/guide.html)

## Why These Tools?
- **100% local**: All processing happens in your browser
- **No upload**: Your files never leave your device
- **No signup**: No account, no email, no tracking
- **Works offline**: Once loaded, works without internet
